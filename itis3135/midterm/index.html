Who

My name is Gabriel Fuller, and my hobby is gaming. Recently, I have been playing Hunt Showdown (covered in later section). With a few of my friends. Online, I am known as Rockst1r or Nerdymob.

What

Recently I have been playing Hunt Showdown, "a high-stakes, tactical PvPvE first-person shooter" - Steam

This game is best described by me as a western-style zombie game where you're primary goal is to escape with your life and hopefully a reward or two.

When

Hunt Showdown initially released February 22, 2018. Even with the game being out for 4 years, I have only been playing for roughly 1 year.

Where

I currently game in the dorms at UNCC on Xbox. This tends to lead to interesting situations such as the fire alarm going off during a match.

Why

Hunt showdown is a difficult game that takes a lot of time and practice. Why play a difficult game? I find it to be very rewarding for many reasons:

  1. High skill cap.
  2. Smaller community. This leads to each player taking the game seriously and an all around enjoyable experience.
  3. Intense gameplay; from great aim to panicking, this is one of few games that can get my heart pumping.

How to improve

To improve in most things it takes patience, practice, and understanding. In videogames, you have to be consistant in how you play and when due to the variablity in different playstyles. If you can learn about the game in any form, you get a greater understanding of how the game functions and how to react in certain situations.
